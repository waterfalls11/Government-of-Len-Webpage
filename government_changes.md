# Updates to the National Government
Following the ratification of the new Constitution, we have implemented a new government structure. This updated Constitution ensures that the Government operates more efficently, transparently, and is more fair to its citizens. This new structutre better equips us to uphold the rights of our citizens, and pass legislation to protect them. Even with these changes, Len citizens won't notice much of a change.
