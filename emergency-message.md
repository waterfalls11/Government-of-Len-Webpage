LEAS ALERT | WINTER WEATHER ALERT

WHERE... The Government of Len has issued a WINTER WEATHER ALERT for the District of Jojo, Grizzly Province, Riley Province, and The Allied Territories of Oakley and Pibble.

WHEN... The temperature will begin dropping at around 18:00 (6:00PM) Thursday, 26 January.

WHAT... Below freezing, and potentially hazardous temperatures are expected to be arrive within the next 48 hours. These temperatures will be dangerous, causing serious temperature related illnesses without proper protection. 

ACTION... Reduce outdoor time as much as possible, and avoid travel. If travel is required, prepare for below freezing temperatures and icy conditions. Remember, any temperature below freezing (0 C, or 32 F) can cause frostbite in as little as 10 minutes to exposed skin.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION... An ACT message will be issued if neccesary.

Government of Len | Office of the Prime Minister
