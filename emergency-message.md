## Severe Weather Bulletin
### 4 Day Outlook 

ISSUED ON... 11 APRIL 2026 @ 07:26
VALID ON... 14 APRIL 2026 @ 00:00

ENRL is testing a new bulletin format. Give feedback to the Government of Len at generalmail@govlen.gay.

RISK... 3/5 CONSIDERABLE RISK. Severe weather events will occur and disrupt day to day life.

DETAILS... The SPC has placed areas southwest of Len under day 1, day 2, and day 3 outlooks. We are under a day 4 outlook.  ENRL meteorologists have low confidence on the storm mode, but some of the threats are clearer. Destructive winds and large hail are possible. The threat for tornadoes will exist on Tuesday, but there is also a conditional threat for a tornado outbreak in the Midwest. Weather there will be discrete supercells will dictate the tornado outbreak risk as well as the location of a surface low and associated troughs. Uncertainty remains on Tuesday.

Be aware that these storms may cause significant impacts to life and property. Take steps to mitigate the impacts of severe weather which develops in the coming days.

INDIVIDUAL THREAT RISKS... Not available.
