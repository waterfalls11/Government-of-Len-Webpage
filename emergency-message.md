## Severe Weather Bulletin
### 5 Day Outlook (on 10 April) // 6 Day Outlook (on 9 April)

ISSUED ON... 09 APRIL 2026 @ 20:06
VALID ON... 14 APRIL 2026 @ 00:00

REVISED... 09 APRIL 2026 08:50 FOR SPC OUTLOOK INACCURACY 

ENRL is testing a new bulletin format. Give feedback to the Government of Len at generalmail@govlen.gay.

RISK... 3/5 CONSIDERABLE RISK. Severe weather events will occur and disrupt day to day life.

DETAILS... The SPC has placed areas southwest of Len under day 3, day 4, and day 5 outlooks. 4-9 day outlooks are only issued with high confidence for an event, and the later an outlook is issued, the more confidence is required to issue. Len and its surroundings are under a day 6 and a day 7 outlook.  ENRL meteorologists have low confidence on the storm mode, but the threats are clearer. Destructive winds, large hail, and tornadoes are possible. 

Be aware that these storms may cause significant impacts to life and property. Take steps to mitigate the impacts of severe weather which develops in the coming days.

In observance of the Day of Silence, ENRL will not issue an outlook on 10 April, Friday. Thank you for understanding.

INDIVIDUAL THREAT RISKS... Not available.

Ace, ENRL
